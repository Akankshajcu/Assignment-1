Goals

Established in 2012, CossiePlay is a locally run family business, with a goal to appeal towards the young audience. CossiePlay wants to expand the business by going online. Apart from the second-hand costumes and an ever increasing amount of CosPlayers from all age groups, CossiePlay aims to have a creative and attractive website primarily aimed for the younger generation, .

Success Evaluation

The website needs have a proper plan and strategy besides being unique as the main target audience is the younger generation. With a clear navigation towards the pages providing useful information, the website will also be attractive. Proper marketing alongwith Social networking and SEO, will help CossiePlay reach the maximum target audience in a short period of time.

Target Audience

Description
The concept of building CossiePlay was to reach out to all age-groups of audience across different areas of Hulkstown, and focussing on younger generations.
Primary Users
Young Generation CosPlayer's renting out for parties.
Secondary Users
Any customer looking to buy costumes for other events and parties.
User Characteristics
- Age : 15+
- Gender: Male and female
- Education: Secondary school++
- Computer and web experience: Online shopping experience
